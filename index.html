Climate & Sustainability Engines

Three engines. Built to drive real outcomes.

Scope 1–3 Climate Intelligence Platform

Scope 1–3 climate intelligence platform

An AI-powered platform that unifies Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 emissions into a single, continuously updated system—moving beyond static reporting to real operational control.

  • Scope 1 (Direct emissions): Track fuel use, on-site operations, and owned assets with high-frequency data integration.
  • Scope 2 (Purchased energy): Monitor electricity and energy consumption across locations with market-based and location-based accounting.
  • Scope 3 (Value chain): Map suppliers, logistics, materials, and downstream usage to identify hidden emission hotspots.
  • AI-powered estimation: Fill data gaps using intelligent models and continuously improve emissions accuracy.
  • Scenario simulation: Test decarbonization strategies—supplier changes, material swaps, logistics optimization—and see impact on emissions, cost, and timelines.
  • Target tracking: Monitor progress against SBTi targets, internal carbon budgets, and regulatory requirements.

The result is a single source of truth for sustainability, finance, and operations teams—turning emissions data into clear, prioritized action.

Outcomes

  • Faster, more accurate inventory for reporting and disclosure.
  • Clear, ranked actions tied to both emissions and business impact.
  • A single climate view for sustainability, finance, procurement, and leadership.
